Blackjack Rules

  • Try to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  • Dealer's total is randomly generated between 16-21.
  • You can "Hit" to be dealt another card or "Stand" to end your turn.
  • If your first two cards match, you can "Split" your hand.
  • Beat the dealer's hand to win your bet.
